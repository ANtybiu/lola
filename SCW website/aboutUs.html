The history of SCW
2000
SCW Founding
SCW's origins can be traced back to here. The original SCW offices were located in an old, dusty shop lot at Bandar Puteri Puchong.
2007
Audit License
SCW can now offer Auditing and Tax services after receiving the long awaited auditing license from MIA.
2012
Relocation
SCW relocated it's offices to the 5th floor of Setiawalk block F-9.
2019
HQ Relocation
Founder Seah Cheong Wei relocated the company's HQ to a new branch at 3Elements Seri Kembangan while the Setiawalk branch remained in operation
2025
Today
SCW is now 25 years old and has 247 total clients and partners.
